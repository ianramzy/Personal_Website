Inspiration

I was playing pong and had just watched Tron (great movie by the way) and decided I wanted to make something Tron themed... and pong seemed simple.

How it's built

I made this entirely in ActionScript 3.0 (flash) and the AI just does a simple calculation to minimize the current distance between the incoming ball and the computer controlled paddle.

Future ideas and what's next

In the future I would like to make the AI controlled paddles more advanced, I know there is a way using some geometry to calculate where the ball will be at the edge of the screen. (Only problem is it may be unbeatable).
